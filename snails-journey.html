A Snail's Journey

Click here to play!

Ring-a-ring-ding-ding! Shelee the snail got a special phone call from her friend Coco. What's this all about, a game of hide-and-seek? Shelee's got to get out of her comfort zone and explore the wilderness.

I designed this narrative Bitsy game that centers around the positive message of friendship. The player’s main goal is to help Shelee uncover her friends' hiding spots. Interacting with colored characters and objects is key in progressing the narrative.

Inspired by my walks in nature, I developed characters and settings based on animals and plants I encountered. I paid special attention to how various spaces can be used in conjunction with the narrative to communicate progress. I included hubspaces (where a room is dedicated to two divergent paths and communicates possible actions), prospect spaces (rock garden that spans two rooms to give the impression of being a gargantuan space), a maze (stimulates player’s thinking), and narrow spaces (small bridge that creates contrast and builds up anticipation for what comes next).
