Star Sprout

Designed by Sharon Ku and Leanne Kate Suen Fa

Link to Code

Star Sprout Village is a world filled with tiny greenhouses hanging magically, like a constellation of tiny individual bubbles. The users can navigate through this space and visit each other.

The user cultivates precious plants in someone else’s garden by sending them loving messages. It is only through continuous collaboration and communication that the garden will be healthy and bloom. We want to empower users to feel good about themselves when they view their progress and give a visual representation of their mutual efforts to develop a relationship with someone else.

Through this project we explore the theme of digital interactions and connectivity in a different way from mainstream social media. As such, we also wanted to remove ourselves from the world of intruisive, attention-grabbing notifications, advertisements, and otherwordly interruptions and distractions. Instead, we want to reflect on what it means to create a space collaboratively with users.

Leanne and I contributed equally in programming Star Sprout Village. We developed both the front and back end. I made the plant and pod illustrations, while Leanne made the seed illustrations and buttons.
